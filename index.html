Services

At Proclus Integration Solutions Pvt Ltd, we specialize in providing end-to-end SAP services tailored to meet your unique business needs. Our team of experienced professionals works closely with you to deliver innovative and future-proof solutions, ensuring your organization stays ahead in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

SAP System Implementation & Integration

We offer comprehensive SAP implementation and integration services, guiding your business through every phase of the project—from initial analysis to full implementation. Whether you’re deploying SAP S/4HANA, ECC, or leveraging SAP Cloud solutions like CPI and BTP, we ensure seamless integration into your existing systems, optimizing your operations and driving value.

Upgrade & Migration Support

Stay current with the latest SAP technologies. We help clients smoothly upgrade and migrate their SAP landscapes, ensuring minimal disruption to business operations. Our experts work hand-in-hand with your team to ensure smooth transitions, whether upgrading to the latest version or migrating to SAP HANA or the cloud.

Anaplan Implementation & Support

We provide specialized services for Anaplan, offering support with the implementation, integration, and optimization of your Anaplan environment. Whether you are automating financial planning, sales forecasting, or supply chain management, we help you leverage Anaplan’s powerful cloud platform to achieve better visibility, faster decision-making, and improved operational efficiency.

App Development

We also offer custom app development services to help you build intuitive and scalable applications that meet your specific business needs. From mobile apps to enterprise-grade solutions, our team leverages the latest technologies to create seamless, high-performance applications that enhance user experience and streamline operations.

SAP BASIS Support & Optimization

Our SAP BASIS specialists ensure that your SAP environment is running at peak performance. We offer comprehensive support, including system analysis, error correction, user support, and performance optimization. With deep expertise in SAP systems and databases (including SAP HANA, Oracle, and MS-SQL), we provide expert guidance to maintain a healthy SAP environment.

Business Process Operation Support

When you face challenges in managing business-critical processes, we step in to help. Whether dealing with personnel shortages or system maintenance challenges, our team ensures your operations continue running smoothly. We support your organization in managing complex processes, ensuring consistent performance and minimizing operational risks.
